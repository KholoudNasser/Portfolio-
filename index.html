FinTech / Humanitarian

Ghaith Global Humanitarian Platform

The Problem: Traditional charity platforms lacked unified, secure, and user-friendly donation flows, resulting in high transaction drop-offs and complex sponsorship tracking.

The Solution: Developed "Ghaith" (منصة غيث), a global, highly scalable humanitarian platform that unifies donations, sponsorships, and campaigns with frictionless payment channels, recurring contribution plans, and interactive Zakat calculators.

My Role & Architecture: Technical Lead & Solution Architect. Designed and built the responsive Next.js & React-powered web application, integrated with secure REST APIs for payment processing (MPGS integration), SMS carrier donations, and automated multi-lingual support (Arabic/English).

Business Impact: Enabled a 40% increase in digital donation volume, seamless international transaction handling, and zero security breaches since deployment.

VR / HealthTech / AI

ADHD-VR Diagnostic & Analytics Platform

The Problem: Clinical ADHD assessments traditionally rely on subjective behavioral observation, lacking objective quantitative metrics for gaze focus, attention span, and impulse control.

The Solution: Designed and built a clinical Virtual Reality suite (ADHD-VR) that collects high-frequency gaze and telemetry data during virtual tasks. It pipes this telemetry in real-time to a web dashboard displaying session statistics, gaze focus graphs, and key clinical indices (GDI, PDI).

My Role & Architecture: Lead Full Stack Developer & AI Engineer. Integrated the VR telemetry data stream with a web backend, built the React-based analytics dashboard utilizing Chart.js, and co-developed the pipeline calculating diagnostic metrics.

Business Impact: Published academic research at the AAAI conference and provided clinicians with a novel, high-accuracy quantitative diagnostic and monitoring system.
